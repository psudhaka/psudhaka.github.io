Prasad Sudhakar

Welcome to my homepage. I am currrently a Research Scientist in the AI for Image Analytics (AIIA) Lab at GE Global Research, Bangalore, India. Prior to this, I spent nearly 3 years in the ICTEAM institute, Universite catholique de Louvain, Belgium, with Prof. Laurent Jacques during my postdoc. I obtained my PhD (2011) from INRIA Rennes - Bretagne Atlantique (associated with Universite de Rennes 1), France, under the supervision of Dr. Remi Gribonval.

Before obtaining my M.Sc. (Engg.) degree (2007) from the Electrical Engineering department, IISc, Bangalore, I spent about 3 years in industry developing signal processing algorithms.

Research

At the AIIA Lab in GE, I solve problems related to medical images using tools from classical image/signal processing and machine learning. While I work on data driven methods, I believe domain knowledge can make a difference in problem solving. Also, my interests are in the development of techniques and algorithms concerning efficient acquisition and processing of high-dimensional signals/data and solving related inverse problems by exploiting their low-dimensional structures.
