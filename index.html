Prasad Sudhakar

Welcome to my homepage. I am currently a Styaff Scientist in the Advanced Technology Group (ATG) at GE Healthcare, Bangalore, India. Prior to this, I spent nearly 3 years in the ICTEAM institute, Universite catholique de Louvain, Belgium, with Prof. Laurent Jacques during my postdoc. I obtained my PhD (2011) from INRIA Rennes - Bretagne Atlantique (associated with Universite de Rennes 1), France, under the supervision of Dr. Remi Gribonval.

Before obtaining my M.Sc. (Engg.) degree (2007) from the Electrical Engineering department, IISc, Bangalore, I spent about 3 years in industry developing signal processing algorithms for video and image processing.

Research

At the ATG in GE, I solve problems related to medical imaging/image processing using tools from classical image/signal processing and machine learning.
